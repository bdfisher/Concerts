Northern Invasion 2015

Northern Invasion is a festival that started in 2015. The event is held at Somerset Amphitheater in Somerset, Wisconsin. Northern Invasion includes some of the biggest metal and rock bands in the world including; Slipknot, Disturbed, Five Finger Death Punch, Korn, Avatar, Rob Zombie, Halestorm, Volbeat, etc. The festival is typically held around May 13, 14, and 15. Northern Invasion has quickly become one of the biggest rock and metal festivals of the year in the USA.
